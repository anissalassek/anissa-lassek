Safe Goods

Safe Goods is a dynamic app revolutionizing dessert ordering for those with allergies. From branding to app design, I injected vibrancy and fun, reflected in its playful pink theme. Crafted in Figma, the prototype showcases seamless ordering experiences, demonstrating my ability to merge creativity with functionality in a visually compelling way. Safe Goods is a testament to my passion for inclusive design and commitment to user-centric solutions.
